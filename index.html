HEALTHCARE INFRASTRUCTURE

Health infrastructure is an important indicator for understanding the health care delivery provisions and welfare mechanism in a country.

GOVENRMENT HOSPITAL BEDS
Bihar has only 1 goverment hospital bed for every 10000 people
Sikkim has 1 goverment hospital bed for every 500 people
There are only 7,13,986 Government Hospital Beds in India. Which means, there is only 1 bed available for 1800 people. The situation is even challenging in Rural India, where there is 1 bed for only 3500 people.
None of the states in India could meet the WHO standard of 5 beds per 1000 people.
